The character first appeared in 1968 in 'Marvel Super-Heroes #13' where she was introduced as a prominent officer in the United States Air Force. Carol Danvers became head of security at Cape Canaveral at the request of NASA, and it was there where she met the Kree soldier Mar-Vell, known as Captain Marvel, with flying and fighting abilities, superhuman endurance and a “seventh sense.” which allowed her to sense when danger was nearby made Carol assume a new identity: at that first moment, she was called Ms. Marvel. In 2012, she took on the codename Captain Marvel in 'Amazing Spider-Man vol. 1 #9', when in the Avengers vs X-Men saga, Danvers took on the responsibility of Mar-Vell, the former Captain Marvel.
